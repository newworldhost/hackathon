About This Weather App

Welcome to Team K&JJJ's Weather App page, your go-to platform for accurate and up-to-date weather information. Get the weather details of your favorite locations, check weather updates based on your current location, and take the time to weigh up the pros and cons of our Northern European climate versus other places around the world. This JavaScript feature project makes use of data called from external APIs to give you realtime details on the weather conditions of your searches each and every time. It can even distinguish the difference between locations of a similar name by simply adding the country code on the end of your location of choice! For example, searching 'Birmingham, UK' compared to 'Birmingham, US' will be sure to provide you data true to your intended input, not that we'd need much guesswork to figure out it's probably warmer in Alabama than England right now!
